
====== Controls ======

    Program:
        Quit: Z

    Motion:
        Forward: W
        Back:    S
        Left:    A
        Right:   D
        Up:      C
        Down:    X
    
    Orientation:
        Roll CCW:   Q
        Roll CW:    E
        Pitch up:   Up
        Pitch down: Down
        Yaw left:   Left
        Yaw right:  Right
    
    Zoom:
        Minify:  -
        Magnify: +
        Reset:   Backspace
    
    Spotlight:
        Cone-:      < (,)
        Cone+:      > (.)
        Softness-:  n
        Softness+:  m
    
    Rendering:
        Toggle toon shading: T
        Toggle wireframe:    F
        Toggle light:        Left click on light source
